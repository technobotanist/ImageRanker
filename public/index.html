Project Description

We are selecting images of student life, Libraries spaces, and visually compelling designs.
Our goal is to highlight real representations of the student population and active, dynamic scenes of students and faculty in the Libraries and on the Brickyard.
The initiative aims to make these spaces feel more human, while spotlighting the history of Libraries initiatives and campus archives.
As the home of University history, we want to present evidence of NC State’s past and student stories.
